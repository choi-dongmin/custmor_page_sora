    Conductor Sora Elisabeth Lee is rapidly establishing herself as a force to be reckoned with. Her 2019-2020 season, her first as a young professional, includes multiple débuts across France with la Philharmonie de Strasbourg, l'Orchestre chambre de Paris at Théâtre du Châtelet, l'Orchestre les Siècles at Cite de la Musique, l'Orchestre Symphonique Région Centre-Val de Loire/Tours and l'Ensemble orchestral Épinal la belle image.


    In June of 2019, Sora Elisabeth graduated with distinction from Conservatoire National Supérieur de Musique et de Danse de Paris where she completed a Master in Orchestral Conducting under the tutelage of Alain Altinoglu. Her training in France was preceded by a Bachelor in Orchestral Conducting from the Munich University of Music and Performing Arts in Munich, Germany with Bruno Weil from 2012-2016.
